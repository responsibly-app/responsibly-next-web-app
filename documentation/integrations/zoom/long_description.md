Responsibly is a performance management platform built specifically for financial agencies. It helps agency leaders keep their advisors accountable, motivated, and consistently productive through gamification, structured check-ins, and real-time analytics.

By connecting Responsibly to Zoom via OAuth, advisors and managers unlock meeting-aware accountability. The integration reads upcoming and recent Zoom meetings to give the platform visibility into client touchpoints, allowing advisors to earn XP points for completed meetings and managers to track meeting consistency alongside other performance indicators — without manual data entry.

**What the Zoom integration does:**
- Displays a live feed of the user's upcoming Zoom meetings inside the Responsibly dashboard
- Shows meeting topic, date, time, duration, and a direct join link
- Meeting activity data contributes to each advisor's XP score and consistency streak
- Agency managers can see at a glance which advisors are keeping up with client meeting cadences and which are falling behind

**Core platform features:**
- XP & Points Engine: Every client call, meeting, and closed deal earns XP. Point rules are fully customizable to align with each agency's specific goals
- Live Leaderboards: Real-time advisor rankings with weekly resets and team-wide visibility that encourage healthy competition
- Consistency Streaks: Visual streak counters help build daily habits and let managers instantly identify who needs support
- Goal & KPI Tracking: Set individual and team targets per cycle, then track revenue goals, client touchpoints, and pipeline milestones against actuals
- Agency Analytics: Performance trend dashboards surface pipeline health and underperforming advisors so leadership always knows where to focus
- Accountability Check-ins: Structured daily or weekly check-ins with private manager notes and escalation flows

**Who Responsibly is for:**
Responsibly is designed for financial agencies — insurance, investment advisory, and wealth management firms — that rely on a team of advisors whose daily activity directly drives business outcomes. It is used by agency managers, team leaders, and individual advisors.

**About Responsibly:**
Responsibly is an independent software company building accountability infrastructure for performance-driven financial teams. Our platform is designed to surface what matters, reduce the friction of reporting, and give advisors a clear, motivating picture of their progress every single day.
