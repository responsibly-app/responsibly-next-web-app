
### ENV Variables
`TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN` = your_bot_token          
- server-side, for hash verification
`NEXT_PUBLIC_TELEGRAM_BOT_USERNAME` = YourBot  
- client-side, for the login widget


---
### Register the Bot:
- Open Telegram and search for `@BotFather`.
- Click "Start" and send the command: `/newbot`.
